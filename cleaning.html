Cleaning

A clean premise will provide more hygienic and productive living and working environment. We can very proudly say that cleanliness is at the heart of everything we do.

We offer a range of cleaning services for domestic, commercial and corporate customers. These include, but are not limited to Office Cleaning, Commercial Cleaning, Bin Cleaning, Carpet Cleaning, One-off deep cleaning, window cleaning, Gutter Cleaning, communal Cleaning and numerous other specialised cleaning types to suit your needs. No matter what your situation is, we are fully equipped to help you with a personal touch. Our customers include Retailers, Business offices, medical facilities, care and residential homes and hospitality industry.

Our staff is experienced in different types of cleaning and possess the requisite know how to perform the services in a safe and secure way. They are aware of health and safety regulations and employ this knowledge during the normal operations.

We offer daily, weekly or one-off deep cleaning as per customer requirement. we also provide cleaning staff for any number of days weekly if there is a need.
