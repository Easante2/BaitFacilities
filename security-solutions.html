Security solutions

Our security services are available across a wide range of sectors. Whether your business is a retail outlet , an industrial site, a distribution centre, an educational establishment, a leisure venue, hotel, or you are looking to secure your home, BAITfm has the practical and cost-effective security solution for you. We are confident we can add value and make a difference to your business.

Being local to the East Midlands region and armed with security industry knowledge of a good few years, we believe that our wealth of local expertise enables us to deliver a better experience for the customers we serve. We constantly monitor the market in order to stay competitive while providing affordable services within most local businesses' budget. We don't subcontract our work to third party specialists and we take full accountability on all our provided services. All our employees are properly vetted using previous employment records and CRB checked.

The services we provide

•Store detective and loss prevention- Shop lifting and theft prevention services.

•Construction and commercial sites security- Construction sites are vulnerable to theft of valueable building materials, plant, machinery, tools electrical wires and metallic component. We conduct risk assessment of your construction site and plan security accordingly alongside recommendations to secure your site.

•Personal protection and residential security- We protect individuals and families from personal attacks, vandalism and stalking. We also provide escorts for high value merchandise, precious assets and goods in transit. We look after your home while you are away, monitor fire and intruder alarms, and escort visitors as instructed.

•Event security and crowd management- We provide crowd management for Leisure events, gigs, concerts, sports events. We also conduct threat and risk assessment for events.

•Universities, campuses, colleges & school security- Our staff have vast experience of dealing with threat of drug crime, knife crime theft, and extremist behaviour in and around universities, colleges and schools.

•Property protection services- We make sure you’re vacant or occupied property is safe, secure and regularly being monitored for criminal damage, vandalism, fly-tipping and asset strippers.

•Remote monitoring- Remote monitoring is a cost effective measure which can reduce your security budget drastically. Most of today's modern surveillance systems can be easily integrated to intruder alarm, fire alarm and access control systems. They can detect, warn. track and identify unauthorized intruders. Whenever one of our clients' cameras identifies any suspicious activity, our operators focus in on the live footage and assess each incident in real time and then notify the response partner or the local authority. If there is an intruder at the premises, our operators send an audio warning informing the intruder(s) that they are being monitored and that if they don't leave, further action will be taken.
